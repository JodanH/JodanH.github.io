Legal Frameworks Affected by WannaCry

Impact
Computer Misuse Act 1990

Addresses unauthorised computer access.

HIPAA (US)

Protects healthcare info privacy.

CISA (US)

Promotes cybersecurity info sharing in the U.S., with limited impact on WannaCry.

So What...? My ideas for future incidents

This is what can be done to further enforce legal pressure on sanctioned nations:

  • International Cyber Laws:

    Establish stronger cross-border legal actions to address cyber threats across nations.

  • Sanction Mechanisms:

    Enable collaborative sanctions to create more impactful deterrents for cyber activities.

  • Global Partnerships:

    Promote intelligence-sharing partnerships to enhance international cyber defense capabilities.
